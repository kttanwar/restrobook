About Us

Restro-Book is a convenient self-service table booking system that can be accessed on any device. Restro-Book customizes the booking process, enabling people to book a table through any device, manage restaurant availability, and reservations. Restro-Book automates the day-to-day activity of a restaurant.

The services provided is food ordering and table reservation management for customers as-well-as the restaurant staff, menu information management and report. Main objective of the system to provide an online platform to the customers to reserve a table in of the desired through any device from any location.
